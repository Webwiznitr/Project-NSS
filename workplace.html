What we do:

Birsamunda Vidyapith was established in 1983 and managed by the department of education. It is located in Rourkela MPL block of Sundargarh district of Odisha. To be precise, it is located outside the NIT campus near CWS hospital. Not only we are involved in providing quality education to the children, we also distribute old clothes among the villagers. The cloth is collected from our professors’ quarters by our group members and this helps needy people in covering themselves in extreme weather conditions and also bring loads of joy to small children as they are unable to get proper clothes to wear.
